Our Story

More than a café —
a landmark.

Tucked right beside the majestic Charminar, Nimrah Café & Bakery has been serving the people of Hyderabad since 1939. What started as a small Irani-style tea shop became the city's most iconic cultural stop.

Every morning, queues of locals, tourists, and food lovers line up for the legendary pairing: warm, flaky Osmania biscuits dunked into freshly brewed Irani chai. It's not just breakfast — it's a ritual.

We serve no distractions, no pretense. Just honest food, honest chai, and honest Hyderabadi warmth.
